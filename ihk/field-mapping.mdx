### What This Guide Covers

**PDF Structure Mapping:**
1. Nummer der Eintragung (Entry Number)
2. Firma (Company Name)
3. Sitz (Registered Office/Address)
4. Gegenstand (Business Purpose)
5. Stammkapital (Share Capital)
6. Vertretungsregelung (Representation Rules)
7. Rechtsform (Legal Form)
8. Tag der Eintragung (Registration Date)

**EVA System Workflow:**
- Search process (critical first step)
- Company data entry
- Director/stakeholder management
- Industry codes
- Capital information
- Review and completion

### Key Field Mappings

- **Firmierung** ← PDF Section 2 (Company Name)
- **Anschrift** ← PDF Section 2 (Address)
- **Beteiligten** ← PDF Section 4 (Directors)
- **Branchenschlüssel** ← PDF Section 2 (Business Purpose)
- **Stammkapital** ← PDF Section 3 (Capital)
