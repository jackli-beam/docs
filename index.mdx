## Process Statistics

Key insights from workshop analysis.

<Note>
  **Daily Volume:** ~140 entries per day for Hamburg
  **Process Priority:** 1. Abmeldungen (fastest) ‚Üí 2. Anmeldungen (most complex) ‚Üí 3. Ummeldungen
  **Search-First Critical:** ~50% of Neueintragungen already exist in system
  **Sequential Processing:** Must complete one date before moving to next
</Note>
